The American Voice

Prince Roger Nelson was an American music icon who was never afraid of speaking his mind.
During his career, Prince did anything in order to show his support for equality. From writing "Slave" on this cheeck, to changing his name to a symbol O(+>.
Prince believed all should be free, you can tell in one of his grammy presentation when he gave the award for best album. Prince stated, "Albums stil matter, like books and Black Lives, Albums Still Matter."
Prince's voice could be heard around the world, even if he never said a thing.
